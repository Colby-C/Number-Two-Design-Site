Death's Door is an app to help ghosts, ghouls, and spirits trapped between death and the afterlife search for a rental house, building, or plot of land to eternally haunt.

This was a tongue in cheek project I made for Halloween. I wanted it to emulate other popular vacation booking apps such as Airbnb and Vrbo.
