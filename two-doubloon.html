APPAREL DESIGN


This was a brand I designed for an online cryptocurrency apparel company. I deisgned everything from their apparel designs to their social media posts. I was given their sketches to bring to life their vision for their apparel. For their social media posts, I was encouraged to experiment with different ideas and themes to find new ways to depict information, that could be interpreted as boring to the common person, in an interesting way. Most of the posts were carousels, so I also had to divide out the information into easy to digest portions.
