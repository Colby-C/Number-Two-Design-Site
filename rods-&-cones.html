Rods & Cones is an eye-themed ice cream shop where you can either have a Rod (ice cream bar) or a Cone (ice cream) and create your own Eyescream with toppings. The name comes from the rods and cones found in your eyes. This was my first time animating a logo and now I'm starting to get the hang of it.
