I created this logo for a sports podcast. I was given full creative control over the direction, so I decided to take route of a literal interpretation since it would offer a fun brand theme. I hid secret details within the podcast cover for the client such as recreating his favorite football team of the Steelers in nugget form and dipped them in the sauces of their respective colors.
