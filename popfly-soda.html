Popfly Soda was another personal project where the name came first. I started this logo a couple of years ago, but it ended up left in logo limbo and eventually forgotten about when I didn't like the original direction I was going with it.

While looking through some of my old files, I stumbled upon it. I decided to blow the dust off it and attempt to revive it. I think the part that troubled me the most during the first attempt was trying to get the proportions of the fingers equal to the palm and all it took was some persistance the second time around in order to solve that issue.
