APPAREL DESIGN


I created this logo for an online cryptocurrency apparel brand. I knew that I didn't want to go the three-pointed crown route, which their previous logo had taken, since that has been done to death. The idea came as sort of a eureka moment. While researching other symbols of monarchies and royalty, I realized that a monarch crown's top is comprised of coin-esque shapes. From there, the pieces just fell into place.
